# APT28

APT28 is a Russian state-sponsored threat group attributed to the General Staff Main Intelligence Directorate (GRU) 85th Main Special Service Center (GTsSS) military unit 26165. Active since at least 2004, APT28 has conducted high-profile campaigns including the 2016 compromise of the Hillary Clinton campaign, Democratic National Committee, and Democratic Congressional Campaign Committee.
