# APT29 (Cozy Bear)

APT29 is a Russian state-sponsored threat group attributed to the [[Foreign Intelligence Service|SVR]] that has operated since at least 2008. They primarily target government networks in Europe and NATO member countries, research institutes, and think tanks, and are notably responsible for the 2015 DNC breach and the 2020 SolarWinds supply chain compromise.

## Overview

- **Attribution**: Russia's Foreign Intelligence Service (SVR)
- **Active Since**: At least 2008
- **Primary Targets**: Government networks (Europe, NATO), research institutes, think tanks
- **Notable Operations**: DNC compromise (summer 2015), SolarWinds Compromise (2020)

## Associated Names

IRON RITUAL, IRON HEMLOCK, NobleBaron, Dark Halo, NOBELIUM, UNC2452, YTTRIUM, The Dukes, Cozy Bear, CozyDuke, SolarStorm, Blue Kitsune, UNC3524, Midnight Blizzard

## Key Operations

### SolarWinds Compromise (2020)
In April 2021, the US and UK governments attributed the [[SolarWinds]] supply chain compromise to the SVR, with public statements citing APT29, Cozy Bear, and The Dukes. Industry reporting also referred to this campaign as UNC2452, NOBELIUM, StellarParticle, Dark Halo, and SolarStorm.
