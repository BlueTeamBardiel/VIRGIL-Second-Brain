# CISA AA23-347A: ALPHV/BlackCat Advisory

CISA cybersecurity advisory regarding the ALPHV (BlackCat) ransomware group. This advisory provides threat intelligence, indicators of compromise, and mitigation guidance for organizations targeted by this threat actor.

## Overview
ALPHV, also known as BlackCat, is a sophisticated ransomware-as-a-service (RaaS) operation targeting critical infrastructure and commercial entities. The group employs advanced techniques including encrypted communications, lateral movement, and data exfiltration prior to encryption.

## Key Details
- **Threat Actor**: ALPHV / BlackCat
- **Advisory ID**: AA23-347A
- **Source**: [[CISA]]
- **Type**: Ransomware-as-a-Service (RaaS)

## Recommended Actions
- Monitor for indicators of compromise (IoCs)
- Implement network segmentation
- Enable multi-factor authentication
- Maintain offline backups
- Review and harden remote access controls
