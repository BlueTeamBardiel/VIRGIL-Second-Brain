# The Hacker News

## What it is
Think of it as the *Wall Street Journal* of the cybersecurity world, but written by practitioners for practitioners. The Hacker News (THN) is a widely-read cybersecurity news and information platform (thehackernews.com) that publishes breaking news on vulnerabilities, data breaches, malware campaigns, and threat intelligence in near-real-time.

## Why it matters
When a zero-day vulnerability like Log4Shell dropped in December 2021, The Hacker News was one of the first outlets to publish accessible technical breakdowns, enabling defenders to understand scope and begin patching before many vendor advisories were even finalized. Security teams routinely monitor THN as part of their threat intelligence feed to maintain situational awareness of active exploitation campaigns.

## Key facts
- Founded in 2010, THN has grown to over 5 million monthly readers, making it one of the highest-traffic cybersecurity publications globally
- Covers CVE disclosures, APT campaigns, ransomware operations, and tool releases — functioning as a practical OSINT source for threat hunters
- Not affiliated with *Hacker News* (news.ycombinator.com), which is a Y Combinator-run tech community board — a common point of confusion on exams and in practice
- Security professionals use THN alongside CISA advisories and vendor bulletins to build a composite threat picture during incident response
- Articles frequently cite technical sources (NVD, vendor blogs, researcher disclosures), making it a useful starting point for deeper primary-source investigation
