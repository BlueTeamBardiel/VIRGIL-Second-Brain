# 3PARA RAT

## What it is  
Imagine a spy who can not only eavesdrop but also lock doors, turn on lights, and walk around unseen—3PARA RAT is that spy. It’s a modular remote‑access trojan that installs itself on Windows targets, giving attackers full desktop control, credential theft, and data exfiltration capabilities.
