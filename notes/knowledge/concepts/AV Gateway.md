# AV Gateway

## What it is
Think of an AV Gateway as a toll booth on a busy highway that checks every car for hidden contraband before letting it through. Technically, it is a network security appliance that sits at the perimeter—often in front of a firewall or proxy—and inspects inbound and outbound traffic for malware using signatures, heuristics, and sandboxing.
