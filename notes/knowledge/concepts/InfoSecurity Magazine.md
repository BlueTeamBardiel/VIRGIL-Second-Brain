# InfoSecurity Magazine

## What it is
Think of it as the *Wall Street Journal* of the cybersecurity industry — a trade publication that reports on breaches, vulnerabilities, and policy developments the way financial journalists track market movements. InfoSecurity Magazine is a leading B2B cybersecurity media outlet (owned by Reed Exhibitions) that publishes news, analysis, threat intelligence reports, and hosts the annual Infosecurity Europe conference.

## Why it matters
During the 2020 SolarWinds supply chain attack, InfoSecurity Magazine was one of the primary outlets synthesizing threat intelligence from CISA, FireEye, and Microsoft into digestible incident timelines for security practitioners. Security analysts use publications like this to maintain situational awareness — understanding the threat landscape without having to read dozens of raw vendor advisories daily.

## Key facts
- Founded in 1994; one of the oldest continuously published cybersecurity trade magazines, predating many modern threat categories
- Publishes the annual **"Infosecurity Europe"** conference in London, a major networking and threat-intelligence-sharing event for security professionals
- Covers compliance frameworks including ISO 27001, GDPR, and NIST CSF — making it useful for practitioners tracking regulatory changes
- Regularly features **Threat Intelligence** reporting relevant to CySA+ exam topics: TTPs, IOCs, and adversary attribution
- Free to access online; operates on a B2B advertising model targeting security vendors and enterprise buyers
