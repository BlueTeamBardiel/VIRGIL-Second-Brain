# BeyondTrust

## What it is
Think of BeyondTrust as a strict hotel concierge who hands out room keys only for the exact room you need, only for as long as your reservation lasts, and logs every door you open. It is a Privileged Access Management (PAM) platform that controls, monitors, and audits privileged accounts — administrator credentials, service accounts, and root access — across enterprise environments.

## Why it matters
In the 2020 SolarWinds supply chain attack, attackers pivoted through networks by harvesting privileged credentials and moving laterally undetected for months. A properly configured PAM solution like BeyondTrust would have vaulted those credentials, enforced just-in-time access, and generated session recordings that could have detected anomalous admin behavior far earlier in the kill chain.

## Key facts
- **Privileged Password Vaulting**: BeyondTrust stores and automatically rotates privileged credentials, eliminating hardcoded or shared passwords — directly mitigating credential theft attacks.
- **Least Privilege Enforcement**: Its Privilege Management component removes standing local admin rights from endpoints, forcing elevation only on approved, audited requests.
- **Session Recording & Monitoring**: Every privileged session can be recorded keystroke-by-keystroke, providing forensic evidence and satisfying compliance requirements (PCI-DSS, HIPAA, SOX).
- **Just-in-Time (JIT) Access**: Temporary privilege grants expire automatically, shrinking the window an attacker has to exploit a compromised account.
- **Remote Access PAM**: BeyondTrust's Secure Remote Access product replaces VPNs with brokered, monitored vendor connections — addressing the third-party access risk vector.
